About The Clean Queen

The Clean Queen is a locally operated cleaning service based in Nanaimo, BC, providing thoughtful, detail‑oriented cleaning for homes and businesses. From routine cleans and deep cleans to move‑outs, Airbnb turnovers, and office spaces, every service is tailored to the needs of the space and the schedule of the client. Founded by Amy, who moved from Florida to Nanaimo in 2009, The Clean Queen was built on a simple idea: cleaning should feel refreshing, reliable, and respectful. We show up on time, communicate clearly, and pay close attention to the details that make a space feel truly finished. Clients choose The Clean Queen for consistency, a calm presence, and results they can count on — whether it’s a family home, a rental property, or a workplace. .
